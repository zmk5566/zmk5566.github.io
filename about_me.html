About Me

Computational media, sound, accessibility, visualization, robotics, and cybernetics.

Currently, I am a PhD candidate in the department of Computational Media and Arts under Prof. Huamin Qu's supervision at Hong Kong University of Science and Technology.

I did my BSc Computer Science degree at University of Birmingham, UK and completed my Master of Fine Arts degree at China Academy of Art.

My interests are often related to sound, accessibility in HCI, visualization, robotics, and cybernetics.

I am a member of Nansha Everything Orchestra (N.E.O.).
